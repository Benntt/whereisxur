Who is Xûr?

Xûr, Agent of the Nine, appears in Destiny 2 every Friday at 5 PM UTC and leaves Tuesday at 5 PM UTC. He offers Exotic and Legendary gear in exchange for Strange Coins.

His location is always the Tower Bazaar, though his inventory changes weekly.
